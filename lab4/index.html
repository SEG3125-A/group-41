About Us

Welcome to Pedal Pro Repair, your trusted partner in keeping your bicycle in peak condition. Founded in the heart of our community, we are a team of passionate cyclists and skilled mechanics dedicated to providing the highest quality bike repair services. At Pedal Pro Repair, we understand the bond between a cyclist and their bike. Whether you rely on your bike for daily commutes, weekend adventures, or professional racing, we're here to ensure your ride is smooth, safe, and enjoyable. Our founder, Alex Turner, launched Pedal Pro Repair with a vision to create a bike repair shop that stands apart through expertise, trust, and community engagement. With over a decade of experience in the cycling industry, Alex and our team of certified mechanics are committed to excellence in every repair, big or small.
